Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies allow us to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Visitor counts
  • Traffic sources
  • User behavior analysis

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

  • Ad personalization
  • Campaign performance
  • Conversion tracking
